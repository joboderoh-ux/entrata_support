The Problem

We are missing dissatisfaction signals until it's too late

Entrata Support handles approximately 10,000 tickets per month. Today, customer dissatisfaction is only surfaced when a formal CSAT score is submitted — which represents a fraction of actual unhappy customers. Frustration expressed in ticket comments, unresolved cases dragging for days with no escalation, and at-risk accounts go undetected until they escalate or churn. There is no systematic way to catch these signals early, prioritize the right tickets, or alert the right people in real time.

The Solution

Automated detection, AI classification, and real-time alerting

The DSAT Intelligence System monitors Zendesk in real time for three types of dissatisfaction signals. When detected, an AI model classifies the root cause, scores severity and churn risk, and generates a structured summary with recommended next actions. High severity cases trigger an immediate Slack alert to the support team.

⭐
CSAT Submission — dsat_submission

Customer submits a low satisfaction score in Zendesk. Triggers immediate AI analysis of the ticket and CSAT comment.

💬
Frustration Expression — dsat_expression

Customer uses a frustration keyword (e.g. "frustrated," "unacceptable," "terrible") in a public ticket comment. Scanned in real time as comments are added.

🔁
Lengthy Discourse — lengthy_discourse

A ticket has been open for 48+ hours with 5+ public replies and no linked development ticket (JIRA). Indicates a case stuck in unproductive back-and-forth with no resolution path.

Data & Privacy

Minimal data footprint — no customer PII stored

✓

No customer PII is stored. Names, emails, and phone numbers are automatically redacted before any data is saved or sent to the AI.

✓

Zendesk remains the source of truth. We store only ticket IDs and metadata. Full ticket content stays in Zendesk.

✓

Temporary processing only. Any comment text used for AI analysis is purged from our database immediately after processing completes.

✓

Encrypted at rest and in transit. Supabase (our database) is SOC 2 Type II certified and handles all encryption.

✓

AI data handling. The Anthropic Claude API does not train on API data submitted via the API under its standard terms.

✓

Access controlled. Database access is restricted to the system only. No broad employee access to raw data.

Current Status & Timeline
